### Domain Focus Strategy
Prioritize by exam weight:
1. **Collaboration Features (30%)** - Spend 30% of study time
2. **Introduction to Git & GitHub (22%)** - Spend 22% of study time
3. **Modern Development (13%)** - Spend 13% of study time
4. **Privacy & Security (10%)** - Spend 10% of study time
5. **GitHub Community (10%)** - Spend 10% of study time
6. **Working with Repositories (8%)** - Spend 8% of study time
7. **Project Management (7%)** - Spend 7% of study time

### Effective Study Methods
- **Read-Practice-Test** cycle for each domain
- **Hands-on practice** > passive reading
- **Create real repositories** and workflows
- **Take notes** in your own words
- **Teach concepts** to others (rubber duck method)
- **Join study groups** or discussions
